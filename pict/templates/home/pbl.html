PICT Study Materials

Welcome to PICT notes!! PICT notes is a free to use Website created by students of PICT to help students attain notes hassle free on a single platform. We strive to provide you quality reference material. You can get books, handwritten notes, presentations, word documents based on your year and subject. We made this site to simplify the process of getting study material and most importantly save your valuable time as this is the stop for everything and anything you need. Except providing study material we also provide you with resources to acquire extra skills like coding for instance. We have also added a section of Stock market as financial literacy is important in this modern age world and keep you up for the challenges of future. With your support it will fuel us to provide you best everytime we update this website. We appreciate you for visiting our website. Thank you!
